Collaborative Learning can be defined as an educational environment which requires or promotes team contributions such that the output is more than the sum of its parts. With this in mind, it is imperative that the attention of this project is less focused on the scope of the members and more focused on the logistics and experience of the objective for collaborative learning.

Why do we care about this?

We are students too, each with our own set of bad experiences that we want to improve on so that others will have a more delightful experience. Education is important to us, one way or another. Almost all of us shifted into our courses because we care about the quality and value of our education above the original incentives offered to us by our previous courses.
